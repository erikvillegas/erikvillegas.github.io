Sidekick for RPAN

Sidekick for RPAN lets you easily browse current broadcasts on the Reddit Public Access Network. Watched a cool stream? Simply tap the star to have the broadcaster's streams pinned to the top or enable notifications to receive a push as soon as they go live! For RPAN moderators, there is even built-in mod tools to make your life easier. Check out the screenshots below!

Features

  • Tap on a broadcast to instantly view it in the official Reddit app.
  • Tap on the star to favorite a broadcaster. Their broadcasts will float to the top of your feed when they are live.
  • Reddit integration: Connect your account to automatically import the users you're following in Reddit.
  • Notifications! Get notified by push instantly when your favorite broadcasters go live.
    • Control whether you want to receive notifications for each broadcaster.
    • Turn off notifications from broadcasters when they stream from particular subreddits.
    • Enable cooldown to avoid excessive notifications when there are back-to-back streams.
    • Set a custom notification tone for each broadcaster.
  • Mod tools! Swipe on a broadcast or use a saved RPAN link in your clipboard to open up these extra moderator-only features.
    • View all comments from a post. Reported, AutoMod-flagged, and removed comments will appear on top for convenience.
    • Tap on a comment to approve, remove, or ban the user that posted it.
    • Tap on the trash icon to remove the stream with removal reason.

More awesome features are being added for users and moderators alike, stay tuned!

  1. On your iPhone or iPad, install Apple's TestFlight app here.
  2. Open TestFlight and accept the Terms and Conditions.
  3. Accept the invite for RPAN Companion by tapping here.

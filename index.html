RPAN Companion (beta) for iOS!


RPAN Companion makes it easy to see who is streaming on RPAN. It's like a slightly prettier version of RPAN Lister. Watched a cool stream? Simply tap the star to have their streams pinned to the top whenever they're next online. You can even enable notifications to receive a push within seconds of the broadcaster going live! To help manage notification chaos, the app provides plenty of configuration options so you can tweak them per your liking.

Features

  • Tap on a broadcast to instantly view it in the Reddit app.
  • Tap on the star to favorite a user. Their broadcasts will float to the top of your feed when they are live.
  • Reddit integration: Connect your account to automatically import the users you're following in Reddit.
  • Notifications! Get notified by push instantly when your favorite broadcasters go live.
    • Control whether you want to receive notifications for each broadcaster.
    • Turn off notifications from broadcasters when they stream from particular subreddits.
    • Enable cooldown to avoid excessive notifications when there are back-to-back streams.
    • Set a custom notification tone for each broadcaster.

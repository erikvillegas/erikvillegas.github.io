App Details

This app will let you easily see what is currently being broadcasted on RPAN, and let you never miss streams from your favorite broadcasters with notifications.

Features

  • Tap on a broadcast to instantly view it in the Reddit app.
  • Tap on the star to favorite a user. Their broadcasts will float to the top of your feed when they are live.
  • Notifications! Get notified by push instantly when your favorite broadcasters go live.
    • Control whether you want to receive push for each broadcaster.
    • Turn off notifications from broadcaster when they stream from particular subreddits.
    • Enable cooldown to avoid excessive notifications from broadcasters with back-to-back streams.
    • You can even set a custom notification sound for each broadcaster!
  • Reddit integration: Connect your account to automatically import the users you're following in Reddit.

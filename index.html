RPAN Companion (beta) install guide for iOS!

This app isn't on the App Store yet, so you'll need to follow the below steps to install it on your iPhone or iPad on iOS 12 or later:
  1. On your iPhone or iPad, install Apple's TestFlight app here.
  2. Open TestFlight and accept the Terms and Conditions
  3. Accept the invite for RPAN Companion by tapping here. You should see the app details and an Install button within TestFlight.

App Details

This very unofficial app will let you easily see what is currently being broadcasted on RPAN.
Features

  • Tap on a broadcast to instantly view it in the Reddit app.
  • Tap on the star to favorite a user. Their broadcasts will float to the top of your feed when they are live.
  • Notifications! Get notified by push instantly when your favorite broadcasters go live. You can configure notifications by subreddit and even set a cooldown period to avoid too many notifications.
  • Reddit integration: Connect your account to automatically import the users you're following in Reddit.
